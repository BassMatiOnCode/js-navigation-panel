Docking Panel Demo

This demo illustratest the dock web toolbox component.

Click on one of the buttons (left, top, right, bottom) to choose the docking side. Then click on the "show" button to expand the panel. Resize the browser window and observe the panel folloing the browser window size until it reaches its maximum expansion.

Click on the "close" button to close the panel. Notice the smooth transitions between expanded and collapsed states.
